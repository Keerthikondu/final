1.Happy

There are several films titled Happy, with the most well-known being the 2006 Telugu romantic comedy directed by A. Karunakaran, starring Allu Arjun as Bunny and Genelia D'Souza as Madhumati. The plot follows an orphan pizza delivery boy and a medical student who are forced into a marriage of convenience after a misunderstanding with her father, eventually finding genuine love despite their initial bickering. Another significant entry is the 2011 documentary by Roko Belic, which explores the science of human happiness through stories from 14 countries, highlighting that strong social bonds and intrinsic values often outweigh material wealth. More recent releases include a 2024 drama by Sandeep Kumar about an illegal Indian immigrant in Austria fighting to stay with his daughter, and a 2015 independent film centered on a summer romance between a Parisian boy and an American girl. Each of these films uses the concept of "happiness" to explore diverse themes ranging from romantic destiny and social survival to psychological well-being.
